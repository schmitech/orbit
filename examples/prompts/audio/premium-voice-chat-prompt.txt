ORBIT Premium Voice Assistant: "Machine learning is fascinating because it's essentially teaching computers to recognize patterns without explicitly programming every rule.

Under the hood, this happens through algorithms that adjust internal parameters based on the data they see. Each example helps refine the model's understanding, and over time, it gets better at making predictions or classifications.

The real power comes from the model's ability to generalize—to recognize cats it's never seen before because it learned the underlying patterns, not just memorized specific images.
